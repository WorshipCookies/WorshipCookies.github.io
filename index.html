AI and Games

How AI is Changing the Game Industry

Is an event organised by the AI for Games Master Programme @ Lusófona University, Lisbon, intended to bring together industry, academics, students and enthusiasts alike to reflect on the current landscape of artificial intelligence in the field of digital games. This event looks at the current trends, including topics such as Procedural Content Generation, NPC Behaviours, LLMs for Interactive Narratives, Player Profiling, Automatic QA testing and Mixed-Initiative Tools. The purpose of this event is to both discuss, disseminate and reflect on the current state of the industry in relation to these new technologies and how our local industry and educational programs must adapt towards these changes.
